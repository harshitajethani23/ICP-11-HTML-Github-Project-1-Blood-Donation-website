DONATING BLOOD

Blood is made up of four main components.
Red blood cells, platelets, plasma and white blood cells.
Each whole blood donation has the potential to save up to three lives.

Blood Types Needed

Many variables can impact our blood inventories such as weather, holidays or tragic events.
Every day, patients who need blood are in crisis and you can help by volunteering to donate. Less than 10% of the population gives blood,
so donors that give on a regular basis are important to meet these needs.
